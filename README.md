# Airbnb Market Insights — Miami

![Dashboard Preview](images/dashboard.png)

## Business Problem

Many people assume that simply listing a property on Airbnb is enough to generate strong returns.  
However, data tells a very different story.

This project explores how factors such as location, property type, pricing strategy, occupancy rates, and host performance directly influence profitability in the Miami Airbnb market.

The analysis revealed that not all properties generate the same level of performance. Revenue distribution changes significantly depending on ZIP code, property characteristics, and operational efficiency.

One of the strongest insights discovered during the analysis was the performance gap between Superhosts and regular hosts. Superhosts consistently achieved higher occupancy rates and significantly stronger RevPAR, suggesting that guest experience, responsiveness, and listing quality have a major impact on profitability.

Rather than focusing only on revenue, this dashboard was designed to provide a broader business perspective on short-term rental performance and investment potential.

---

## Key Insights

- Superhosts achieved significantly higher occupancy rates compared to regular hosts
- RevPAR performance was considerably stronger for Superhosts
- Revenue concentration varied heavily across Miami ZIP codes
- Property type strongly influenced pricing and revenue generation
- High ADR alone did not guarantee stronger overall performance
